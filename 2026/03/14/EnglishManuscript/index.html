英语面试

一、原稿

二、可能问题

2.1 板块一专业能力（技术细节与抗压能力）

这部分导师最看重你是否真的亲手做了项目，而不是“拼凑”出来的。

  1. State Machine Logic: “You mentioned designing state machines. How many states did your vending machine have, and how did you handle the ‘change giving’ (找零) logic?”
    • （考查：逻辑严密性）
  2. Hardware vs. Software Debugging: “In an FPGA project, what is the main difference between hardware debugging and software simulation in your experience?”
    • （考查：对调试手段的理解，如 SignalTap 或波形仿真）
  3. The PS/2 Issue: “Why exactly was the PS/2 protocol ‘unscientific’ as you mentioned? Was it a timing issue (时序问题) or something else?”
    • （考查：你对那个 2014 年博客内容的消化程度）
  4. Verilog Best Practices: “During the all-night coding session, what was the most difficult bug to fix? How did you finally identify it?”
    • （考查：解决问题的具体方法论）
  5. Future Application: “If you were to upgrade this vending machine today, what new features or communication protocols would you add?”
    • （考查：前瞻性和对新技术如 USB/Type-C 的了解）

2.2 板块二——爱好与成电情缘（稳定性与综合素质）

这部分导师想看你是否真的想留在成都，以及你的性格是否适合高强度的科研。

  1. Travel and Research: “Traveling requires good planning. How do you apply your ‘planning skills’ from traveling to your engineering studies?”
    • （考查：迁移能力）
  2. Adapting to Chengdu: “You love snow, but it rarely snows in Chengdu. Will you feel disappointed if you stay here for three years and only see rain?”
    • （一个幽默的小坑，考查你的临场应变）
  3. Handling Challenges: “You said you’re not frustrated by new challenges. Can you give another example of a non-academic challenge you’ve faced?”
    • （考查：逆商/AQ）
  4. Why this School: “You saw our building in March. Besides the ‘vibe,’ what specific research direction in our School of Info and Comm Engineering attracts you most?”
    • （重点： 考查你对该学院实验室/导师的研究方向是否做过功课）
  5. The Ginkgo Avenue: “If you become a student here, who would you like to walk with on the golden Ginkgo Avenue at the end of the year?”
    • （闲聊题，看你的社交态度和性格是否阳光）

2.3 板块三——家乡美食（沟通能力与表达技巧）

这部分导师其实在考察你的英语口语流利度，以及你如何向外行解释复杂事物。

  1. Cultural Pride: “Leizhou is not as famous as Shenzhen. What do you think is the biggest advantage of growing up in a less developed but culturally rich place?”
    • （考查：思想深度和价值观）
  2. Describing Flavors: “You mentioned ‘Chao Hefen.’ Can you describe the cooking process or the specific ‘secret’ that makes it taste so different?”
    • （考查：瞬间组织英文描述复杂过程的能力）
  3. Promoting Your Home: “If you were the mayor of Leizhou, what is the first thing you would do to make it as famous as the other two peninsulas?”
    • （考查：大局观和创造力）
  4. Handling Skepticism: “When your friends were skeptical about your hometown, how did you feel? Did you feel offended or just eager to prove them wrong?”
    • （考查：情绪管理）
  5. Seafood and Engineering: “Is there any similarity between ‘finding the best seafood’ and ‘finding the best solution for a coding problem’?”
    • （这是一个脑洞题，看你的思维跳跃和联想能力）
